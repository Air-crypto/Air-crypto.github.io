Aurora Borealis - May 10, 2024

Aurora borealis as seen from the University of Wisconsin-Madison campus (Porter Boathouse dock and Memorial Union Terrace). Photos taken on my iPhone 15 Pro with 5-10 second exposure lengths.

Solar Eclipse - April 8, 2024
