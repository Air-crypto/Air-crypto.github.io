Experience

January 2024 - Present : Undergraduate Researcher at MIMRTL

Since January 2024, I have been actively involved as an undergraduate researcher at the Molecular Imaging / Magnetic Resonance Technology Lab at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research primarily involves performing model surgery on the LLaVA model to develop cross-modal machine learning applications. I also focus on running model training algorithms at the Center for High Throughput Computing, where I utilize 80GB A100 GPUs to effectively retrain LLMs (such as GPT4) on new datasets while preserving knowledge from previous data. Utilizing Docker, I containerize model dependencies to facilitate collaboration and ensure consistent environment setups among my team members. This role has been instrumental in advancing my skills in innovative machine learning solutions and contributing to the broader field of cross-model machine and transfer learning.

June 2023 - August 2023 : Internship at Theom Inc.

During the summer of 2023, I had the opportunity to intern as a Software Developer at Theom Incorporated, a firm specializing in data security. My main project was the implementation of a fuzzy hashing algorithm, which played a critical role in tracing the lineage and storage locations of data across cloud platforms. This algorithm was crucial for detecting slight modifications or segmentations in data, thus enhancing our ability to secure client data. I also engaged in weekly meetings with my mentor, where I provided comprehensive project reports and updates. This experience not only honed my technical skills but also reinforced my commitment to diligent and reliable work in professional environments.
